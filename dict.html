Theroan Dictionary v2

Here's a dictionary for Theroan. It is pretty long, so I recommend using the "find" tool in your web browser.

These are the abbreviations used in the dictionary:

  • n. - noun
  • v. - verb
  • adj. - adjective
  • adv. - adverb
  • pron. - pronoun
  • adp. - adposition
  • conj. - conjunction
  • interj. - interjection

Each dictionary entry is in this format:

  • word class definition

    root words

  • an n. place

  • annasem n. god

    from annas, suffix -em

  • annas1 n. sky, heaven

  • annas2 adj. blue

  • at conj. and

  • awtan v. to jump

  • ejudagan n. volcano

    from ent + judagan

  • elelim n. food

  • ent n. fire
